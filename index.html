Who We Are

Auramaze is a futuristic tech studio based in Kerala, focused on crafting immersive, interactive, and intelligent digital experiences.

We specialize in:

  • AR/VR Development – Mixed reality apps, interactive XR prototypes, Vision Pro-ready experiences
  • Game Development – Unity-powered 2D, 3D, and multiplayer games for mobile, desktop, and WebGL
  • Custom Software Solutions – Full-stack product development, Unity SDKs, real-time applications
  • 3D Visualization & Simulations – Realistic virtual environments for education, healthcare & engineering

At Auramaze, we combine creativity with code to deliver scalable tech that amazes. Whether you're a startup building your MVP or an institute embracing AR/VR in education — we can help you innovate faster.

We also run workshops, community sessions, and provide consulting for Unity, XR, and simulation projects.
